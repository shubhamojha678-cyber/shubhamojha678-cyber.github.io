The Advocate

Shubham Ojha

Shubham Ojha is an advocate practicing primarily at the Jodhpur Bench of the Rajasthan High Court. He represents individuals, families and small businesses across civil, property, family and criminal matters — with a reputation for disciplined preparation and direct, strategic advice.

The firm's practice emphasises thorough groundwork at the trial stage so that matters carried up on appeal are argued from a strong record. Clients retain the firm for its willingness to say "this is the right course" rather than what is easy to hear.

  • Practice before Rajasthan High Court & Jodhpur District Court

  • Consultation by appointment only
Practice Areas

What the firm handles

Case selection is deliberate. The firm accepts instructions where the work matches the firm's preparation standard and where the client values a direct, long-view approach.

I.

Property Law

Title verification, partition, adverse possession, sale-deed and gift-deed disputes, builder-buyer disputes, revenue court matters, and writ petitions on land records.

II.

Family Law

Divorce, maintenance under Section 125 CrPC, child custody, domestic violence cases, matrimonial property, and inheritance / succession disputes.

III.

Civil Litigation

Contract disputes, specific performance, recovery suits, injunctions, company law matters, and civil revisions and second appeals before the High Court.

IV.

Criminal & NDPS

Bail applications, anticipatory bail, quashing petitions under Section 482 CrPC, NDPS Act defence, and criminal appeals before the High Court.

V.

Writ & Constitutional

Writ petitions under Articles 226 and 227, service matters, public interest litigation, and challenges to administrative orders affecting fundamental rights.

VI.

Arbitration & ADR

Domestic arbitration, Section 34 challenges, Section 9 interim relief, mediation, and conciliation — particularly in commercial and family disputes.
