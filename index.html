About the Firm

Principled Advocacy at Rajasthan's Premier Court

Shubham Ojha & Associates is a dedicated legal practice based at the Rajasthan High Court, Jodhpur Bench. Advocate Shubham Ojha brings deep expertise across property disputes, family law, civil litigation, criminal defence, NDPS matters, and service law.

The firm is built on a foundation of integrity, thorough legal research, and client-centred advocacy. Every case is approached with the seriousness it deserves — whether a straightforward contractual dispute or a complex constitutional matter.

With meaningful courtroom experience spanning the High Court and District Courts of Jodhpur, the firm has successfully represented clients across a wide spectrum of legal challenges.
